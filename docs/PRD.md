# AINEX Enterprise AI Operating System
## Product Requirement Document v1.0

## 1. Product Vision
AINEX OS is an Enterprise AI Operating System that helps companies build AI Workers, automate workflows, analyze business data, and connect internal systems.

## 2. Core Positioning
AINEX OS is not a chatbot.
AINEX OS is a Business Brain + AI Workforce + Automation Platform.

## 3. Target Users
- Listed companies
- Enterprise management teams
- Sales departments
- Finance departments
- HR departments
- Operations teams
- SMEs preparing for digital transformation

## 4. MVP Modules
### 4.1 Business Brain
AI analyzes business data and gives executive-level recommendations.

### 4.2 AI Workers
AI Workers include:
- CEO Worker
- Sales Worker
- Finance Worker
- Customer Support Worker
- Document Worker

### 4.3 Workflow Automation
Powered by n8n for:
- Email automation
- Report generation
- Lead follow-up
- Invoice reminders
- Inventory alerts

### 4.4 Knowledge Base
Companies can upload documents, SOPs, product catalogs, contracts, and reports for AI to answer questions.

## 5. MVP Goal
Build a working demo where the AI can:
1. Read sales data
2. Read inventory data
3. Read invoice data
4. Generate CEO report
5. Send report by email
6. Trigger workflow from n8n

## 6. Sprint 1 Scope
- Docker environment
- n8n running
- Google Sheet demo data
- OpenAI integration
- CEO Daily Report workflow
